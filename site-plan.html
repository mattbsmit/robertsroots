Content

Home page

The home page is going to be the introduction and description of what the website is going to be about. It'll include mainly the logo with the option to scroll down and meet the goal and the author behind the site. As my grandma wrote as her purpose behind the blog that I'm going to replace, "This blog is specifically for the decendants of Hugh Roberts(1) and Margaret Roberts(2)." and "Share & exchange information about the Roberts family, Keep in touch with the Roberts family members. Locate and find all living descendants of Hugh & Margaret Roberts, Inspire & motivate others to become involved in the Roberts family research, Share knowledge, resources, and information about how to do genealogy"

Images for the Home page

Articles

The artile page of this site is where I going to move all the articles that my grandma has on her blog and bring them over to this site. It consists of sharing birthdays of family members that have passed away, sharing important dates for my family, and letting my family learn about our past. The hardest part of this page is going to be taking all the posts from 2009 - 2022 and moving them over.
